About

I'm Uri González, member of the Laboratory 25 at the School of Psychology, UNAM. I'm currently doing a short research internship at the University of Birmingham and the Nottingham Trent University under the guidance of Dr Massimiliano Di Luca and Dr Darren Rhodes.

My research interests are focused in the study of temporal perception, specifically in Bayesian timing and temporal decision making. For that reason, another large part of my interests are focused in psychophysics, sensory integration, Bayesian modeling and Bayesian inference.
